Abdullah Nazir

Postdoctoral Fellow at Hong Kong Center for Construction Robotics, by courtesy at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I'm interested in robotic manipulation. Specifically, the development of robot hands and planning/control software to skillfully handle objects for applications in domestic and industrial scenarios.


Please do reach out for any possible collaboration in the areas of manipulation, loco-manipulation, etc.
